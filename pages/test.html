Seaside Paradise

Explore the Beaches of Tangalle!

Tangalle is a beach lover's paradise, where golden sands and turquoise waters stretch for miles. These serene shores, bordered by swaying palms, offer an ideal escape for relaxation or adventure. Whether you want to sunbathe, surf, or explore hidden coves, Tangalle’s beaches promise unforgettable moments and stunning sunsets. Unwind in this tropical haven and let the soothing ocean waves be your backdrop.

Turtle Experiences

Experience Sea Turtles Nesting on the Shore!

Discover the incredible experience of witnessing sea turtles nesting along the shore. Witness these magnificent creatures as they come ashore to lay their eggs in the sand, a breathtaking sight during early mornings or at night. Learn about the nesting process, the challenges these turtles face, and the importance of conservation efforts to protect their habitats. Join guided tours that educate and inspire appreciation for these magnificent marine animals while ensuring their safe journey back to the ocean.

Beauty of Tangalle

Embrace the Coastal Paradise of Tangalle!

Tangalle is a coastal gem in southern Sri Lanka, where nature’s splendor unfolds along pristine beaches, ush landscapes, and tranquil lagoons. The town’s serene beaches—bordered by swaying coconut palms and kissed by the turquoise waves of the Indian Ocean—invite you to relax and embrace the laid-back tropical vibe. Tangalle’s allure goes beyond its shoreline, with rich mangroves, hidden coves, and biodiverse jungles offering endless exploration. The area’s natural beauty is complemented by its warm, welcoming community and rich cultural heritage, making Tangalle a must-visit for those seeking a peaceful yet vibrant escape.
